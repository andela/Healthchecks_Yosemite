General questions

How do you create a check? (Video available)

  • Log into your account
  • Click on add check. A check will be created
  • Click on the period to activate a modal
  • Set the timeout period and grace period to your prefered interval
  • save.

How to delete a check? (Video available)

  • Log into your account
  • Click on the settings icon. A dropdown will appear
  • Click remove. A modal will pop up warning you that you can't undo changes
  • click on remove.You check will be removed
  • save.
{% video 'https://youtu.be/mAJ4U9bFryk' '600x300' %}

Can you change the name of a check (Video available)

  • once you have created a check, its default name is will be unnamed
  • click on unnamed. A modal will pop up and you can fill a friendly name
  • Click remove. A modal will pop up warning you that you can't undo changes
  • Optionally, you can assign tags for easy filtering. Separate multiple tags with spaces.
  • click save. Your check would be renamed
{% video 'https://youtu.be/irlbjU3h1Mo' '600x300' %}

Can healthchecks send alerts to multiple email addresses?(Video available)

Yes You Can.

  • Scroll to the top of the page and click on the Integrations tab
  • You will be redirected to the Integrations page
  • Here you will find a list of channels you can use to recieve notifications.
  • Scroll down to email and click on Add Integration
  • After entering an email address, healthchecks.io will send out a confirmation link. Only confirmed addresses will receive notifications.
